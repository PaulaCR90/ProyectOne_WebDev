OUR MANUFACTURER

Heckaman Homes is a manufacturer of custom modular homes, also known as "off-site" or "systems-built" homes. Our homes are built to meet state and local building codes. Heckaman offers ranch, cape cod, two story and multi-family housing and specialize in customized floor plans. The average home size ranges from 850 to over 3,000 square feet. Established in 1969, Heckaman Homes is one of the oldest modular home manufacturers in the Midwest having built more than 20,000 houses from its original location in Nappanee, Indiana.
